My History

Practicing psychology is one of my greatest pleasures and most fulfilling pursuits. I would like to share how my career started and the theoretical foundations of my work.

Determining one’s career path usually involves starting out on a completely different road. I chose the path that excited me, I excelled in and last, the one that feels most “home” to me. That describes my career journey. While I was in my nursing school, I much preferred the therapeutic conversations that I had with my patients over the direct medical care that I administered. After much anxiety I changed my major and did my research. I decided that I would pursue a doctorate in psychology. My dream was to develop a treatment program in which many people’s lives would be equipped with positive practical and meaningful coping skills that would allow them to live at their highest potential. It would be based on strengths as opposed to weaknesses.

More than thirty years have passed since this dream began. You may wonder, to what extent does reality mirror my dream? The answer is partly. The vision I had for my future contributed only partly to the forces that actually shaped my career and my life. The older you are, the more you humbly realize the truth of this. I am immeasurably grateful for my career. I enjoyed a thriving practice in which other mental health providers and I served large numbers of people in the Tampa Bay area.. I have reached thousands of people with meaningful information about emotional, social, and spiritual health on the radio. I currently have the privilege of serving Veterans at Bay Pines VA Health Care System. I also train VA employees to be more effective health coaches and to connect better with each other and with Veterans.

My small private practice is like coming home. I develop close relationships with my patients and utilize my experience, skills, knowledge and evidence based treatments to promote meaningful psychological and social healing. My patients are able to receive individualized care because I maintain a small practice which allows me to devote my very best to them.

My psychotherapy practice is informed by an ever-growing body of literature that I continually refer to. A variety of therapeutic approaches form the foundation of my work. These include cognitive behavioral therapy, psychodynamic therapy, exposure response prevention, meditation/mindfulness, solution-focused therapy, and systems theories, and imago therapy. The art/science of psychotherapy calls for the practitioner to select the most relevant approach that will have the greatest positive impact on ones’ patient(s). The most important factor associated with success however is the trusting relationship between the therapist and the patient.

Offered Services

Anxiety

When anxiety affects the quality of life of an adult or child, the earlier the intervention, the better the prognosis. Dr. Palcan provides assistance with panic attacks, phobias, social anxiety, OCD, and PTSD.

Mood Disorders/Depression

Dr. Palcan assists people who experience depression due to grief/loss, medical issues, bipolar disorders and other life circumstances.

Coping with Medical Conditions

The most common health problems in the United States like diabetes, heart disease, stroke, cancer, and arthritis and many other medical issues, require self management skills in order to live as fully as possible.

Shared medical appointment for chronic and acute medical conditions

Drs. Palcan and Figueroa provide a dynamic and comprehensive group appointment that provides medical treatment for both chronic and acute health conditions as well as the highest quality education and guidance in self management. comprehensive treatment approach to address.

Weight Loss

Along with the medical expertise of Dr. Figueroa, clients receive a comprehensive, psychological/medical treatment program that results in weight loss results.

Tobacco Cessation

Nicotine addiction is one of the most challenging, if not, THE most challenging addictions to successfully eliminate. Dr. Palcan provides individual tobacco cessation treatment. Within four sessions, many clients are tobacco free.

Men's Issues

Men and women often have different needs, different ways of manifesting psychological stress and different preferences for solutions. Dr. Palcan assists men with career problems, relationships, psychological stress, addictions, and much more. Her approach is more solution-focused and involves skill building, and active problem solving.

Adolescence

Dr. Palcan builds a bridge between parents and youth, promoting understanding and communication that results in mutual respect. She works with adolescents who experience self-destructive behavior, lying, cheating, academic problems, negative attitudes, sibling or peer problems, violent relationships, drugs and alcohol, depression/anxiety, and sexuality issues.

Relationship Therapy

All healthy relationships have their trials. Power struggles, communication problems, sexual dissatisfaction, parenting discrepancies, premarital counseling and infidelity are some of the most common problems that Dr. Palcan provides valuable assistance for. Couples focus on their interaction as well as self-improvement and self-awareness.

Family Therapy

Families seek counseling in order to generate understanding and solutions. Divorce, behavioral/academic issues, conflict, addictions, abuse, or the death or illness of a member often bring a family in for treatment with Dr. Palcan.

Christian Psychotherapy

Dr. Palcan provides psychotherapy services to individuals across the entire dimension of faith, from those who are not spiritually interested to those who are devout practitioners within a religious communities. Dr. Palcan is specifically trained in the area of Christian Psychotherapy. Individuals who prefer actively incorporating Christian values and principles within their treatment, may request this specificity and find great value in Dr. Palcan’s treatment.
