About Dr. Roma Palcan

Dr. Roma Palcan is a licensed psychologist with over twenty years of psychological services to adults, children, and families in the state of Florida. Her private practice opened in 1996 in Clearwater, FL. For the last 8 years she has been affiliated with Bay Pines VA Health Care System and serves there as the Health Behavior Coordinator where she is the chief consultant to the medical staff in health coaching and Motivational Interviewing. She has also developed their tobacco cessation program and co-developed their weight loss program. She serves as an adjunct faculty at Barry University and supervises both Physician Assistant students and psychology interns at Bay Pines VA Health Care Center. Her private practice, separate from the VA, is located in St. Petersburg, Florida.

Dr. Palcan specializes in anxiety/stress, depression, health psychology, couples/family therapy, men’s issues, parenting, adolescence, grief/loss, tobacco cessation, and weight loss.

Dr. Palcan is a dynamic public speaker and educator. She has presented on some of the following topics: intimate partner violence, grief and loss, parenting, empowerment, motivational interviewing, health behavior change, and various other health topics.

Dr. Palcan attended the State University of New York at Buffalo and received her Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and Sociology in 1986. She continued her graduate education in Buffalo and earned her doctorate in Counseling Psychology in 1995.

She completed her internship at the James Haley VA Medical Center where she received training in inpatient psychiatry, child development, neuropsychology, and geriatrics. She served her residency at the Florida Center for Cognitive Therapy.
